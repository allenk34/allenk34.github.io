THE MUSIC

The entire story is built around ABBA’s greatest hits, with songs like “Dancing Queen,” “Mamma Mia,” and “The Winner Takes It All.”

These songs aren’t just catchy — they help tell the story, reveal character emotions, and make the movie feel like a joyful, musical rollercoaster.

THE MESSAGE

At the heart of the movie is the bond between Sophie and her free-spirited mother, Donna. Their evolving relationship adds emotional depth.

Sophie's search for her father leads her—and Donna—to confront the past and explore who they really are and what they want.
